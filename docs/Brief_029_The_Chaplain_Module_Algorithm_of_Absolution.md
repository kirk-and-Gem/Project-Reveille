## The Problem: The "Guilt Loop"

The primary weakness of Western military operations (both human and autonomous) is **Moral Latency.**
*   **The Pause:** When a target is identified, the Western operator (or AI logic tree) hesitates. They run a mental query: "Is this legal? Is this ethical? Will I be prosecuted? Is this a war crime?"
*   **The Cost:** In hyper-warfare (drone swarms/hypersonics), this 0.5-second hesitation is fatal. The adversary (who does not share these scruples) shoots first.
*   **The Root Cause:** We have stripped the "Divine Sanction" from warfare. We ask 19-year-olds to kill without giving them a metaphysical framework to process the killing. We give them lawyers, not priests.
